Basis Phoenix CTF notes (for builders)

This is just for us while building.

Flag format: BASIS{...}
Target event time: 45-90 min
Challenge count: 12 total (including bonus)

Difficulty + points:
- Easy x4 (50 points)
- Medium x5 (100 points)
- Stretch x2 (150 points)
- Bonus x1 (200 points)

Hint timing idea:
- Hint 1 after 5 min (no penalty)
- Hint 2 after 10 min (-10%)
- Hint 3 after 15 min (-20%)

Status words:
- TODO_ASSETS = still needs files
- READY_FOR_TEST = playable, needs testing
- LOCKED = final
